About Me

Ever since I was a kid, I've been obsessed with how things work. My first laptop and phone weren't just gadgets—they were gateways to a world of curiosity.

I'd tear things apart, much to everyone's dismay, just to see what made them tick. I still remember lingering at a radio repair shop, fascinated by the tiny PCB boards and capacitors, firing off questions the mechanic probably couldn't answer.

That relentless curiosity never left me. Today, I channel that same energy into teaching power electronics, leading EV repair, and collaborating on global projects.

I'm still that kid with too many hobbies and not enough time, but now I get to share what I've learned along the way. My journey isn't just about tech—it's about chasing the thrill of discovery and turning it into something meaningful.
